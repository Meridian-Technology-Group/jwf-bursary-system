# 05 — Tour of the dashboard

Backlink: [[README#Filling in the application]]

The dashboard is the first page you see when you sign in. It tells
you at a glance where you are with your application.

## What you'll see

At the top of the page:

- **Welcome back, *(your first name)*** — a friendly greeting.
- A subtitle reading e.g. **2026/27 Assessment Round — continue
  your bursary application below.**

### Application status card

A white card with rounded corners showing:

- **Application status** label at the top.
- The round name (e.g. *2026/27 Assessment Round*).
- A coloured **status badge** — for a brand new application this
  will show **Draft**.
- A progress bar reading **Sections complete — X of 10**, with a
  gold-coloured bar showing how far through you are.

### Quick actions

Two clickable cards underneath:

- **Continue Application** — picks up wherever you left off in the
  form. Tap this to enter section 1 (or whichever section you saved
  last).
- **View Status** — opens the more detailed status page that shows
  the full progress timeline. See [[23-check-application-status]].

### Sidebar (desktop) / menu (mobile)

On the left of the page (or behind the menu icon on a phone) is
a list of the ten sections of the form, each with an indicator:

- A **grey circle** — not started yet.
- A **gold bar** with a fraction — partially filled.
- A **green tick** — complete.
- An **amber warning** — saved but has one or more issues to fix.

You can click any section in the sidebar to jump straight to it —
you don't have to fill them in in order.

## What the dashboard looks like before you have an application

If you've accepted your invitation but haven't yet started the
form, you'll see a card titled **Let's set up your application**
asking you to confirm school and child's name. After that one-time
confirmation, the dashboard switches to the status-and-progress
view described above.
