# 09 — Section 4: Dependent Children

Backlink: [[README#Filling in the application]]

List every dependent child in your household, including the child
this application is for and any siblings. The Foundation uses this
to understand the family's overall financial commitments and any
bursaries / school fees already in play.

## Steps

1. The section starts with a button: **Add dependent child**.
   Click it.
2. A dialog appears titled "Add dependent child" (or "Edit
   dependent child" when editing). Fill in:
   - **Child's name** — full legal name.
   - **School** — the school they attend (or "Not yet in school").
   - **Is this the child this application is for?** — tick this on
     the row for the child named in section 1. Leave it unticked
     for siblings.
   - **Any unearned income for this child?** — e.g. interest from
     accounts in their name. Enter a yearly figure, or 0.
   - **Receiving a bursary / fees paid by someone else?** — enter
     the yearly amount, or 0.
3. Click **Save** in the dialog. The row appears in the table on
   the page.
4. Repeat **Add dependent child** for each additional child.
5. To change a row, click the pencil icon. To delete a row, use
   the trash-can icon.
6. Click **Save and Continue** at the bottom of the section.

## Tips

- Make sure the **named child** tickbox is set on exactly one row
  — the one whose name matches section 1.
- If a sibling already has a bursary at Trinity or Whitgift,
  declare the bursary amount here — it's used in the family-wide
  calculation.
