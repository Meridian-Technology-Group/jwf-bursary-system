# 10 — Section 5: Dependent Elderly

Backlink: [[README#Filling in the application]]

Tell the Foundation about any elderly relatives you support — both
those living with you at home and those whose care you pay for in
a care home.

If you don't support any elderly relatives, this section takes
about ten seconds: just answer "No" twice.

## Steps

### Elderly dependants at home

1. **Do you have any elderly dependant that you are providing for
   at home?** *(required)* — Yes or No.
2. If **Yes**:
   - **How many?** — type the count.
   - For each one, a small block appears asking for their name,
     relationship to you, and approximate annual cost of support.

### Elderly dependants in care

3. **Do you have any elderly dependant in care that you are
   contributing financially towards?** — Yes or No.
4. If **Yes**:
   - **How many?** — type the count.
   - For each, fill in the name, the care home name (if known),
     and the amount you contribute per year.

### Save

5. Click **Save and Continue**.

## Tips

- Estimate annual costs to the nearest £100. Assessors aren't
  looking for exact figures here — they want to see the scale of
  the commitment.
- If anyone in this section is also a member of the household
  whose ID you uploaded in section 2, that's normal — section 2
  is about identity, this section is about financial support.
