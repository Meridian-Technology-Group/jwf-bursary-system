# 13 — Section 8: Assets & Liabilities

Backlink: [[README#Filling in the application]]

Property, vehicles, savings, investments, debts — and crucially
**three months of bank statements** for each parent.

## Before you start

Gather:

- Your council tax bill (current year).
- Three months of bank statements for each parent's main current
  account.
- Any property valuations, mortgage statements, share / ISA
  statements, and details of any loans or hire-purchase
  agreements.

## Sections within the form

### Main residence

- Property type (owned, mortgaged, rented, other), estimated
  value, outstanding mortgage, monthly mortgage payment.

### Other properties

- **Do you own any other properties?** — Yes or No. If Yes, a
  block appears for each property: address, value, mortgage
  outstanding, annual rent received.

### Vehicles, savings, shares

- Approximate total values for vehicles, savings accounts, ISAs
  and shares, premium bonds, and other investments.

### Loans and hire purchase

- **Do you have hire purchase or other loans?** — Yes or No. If
  Yes, list each: provider, outstanding balance, monthly payment.

## Required uploads

- **Council tax bill** — always required. One slot.
- **Bank statements — Parent 1** — three months minimum, uploaded
  as separate files into the same slot. See
  [[17-upload-bank-statements-multi-file]].
- **Bank statements — Parent 2** — shown unless you ticked "sole
  parent" in section 3. Same three-months rule.

## Steps

1. Work top-to-bottom through the form, answering each Yes / No
   question and filling in the conditional fields that appear.
2. Upload the council tax bill.
3. Upload three or more bank statement files into the Parent 1
   slot, and (if applicable) the Parent 2 slot.
4. Click **Save and Continue**.

## Tips

- Use rough valuations — Zoopla/Rightmove estimates are fine for
  property; round savings to the nearest £100.
- Bank statements: any three consecutive months from the last six.
  PDF straight from your bank's app is the cleanest format.
- If you have offset mortgages or unusual products, mention them
  in [[14-section-9-additional-information]].
