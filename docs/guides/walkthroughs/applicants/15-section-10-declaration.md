# 15 — Section 10: Declaration

Backlink: [[README#Filling in the application]]

The last step. Read the declaration, tick the box, type your
name, and submit.

You can only reach the Declaration after the review page has
cleared all issues — see [[21-pre-submission-review-page]].

## What's on the page

### Declaration text

A numbered list of ten statements, including (paraphrased):

1. The information provided is accurate and complete to the best
   of your knowledge.
2. Income and assets are truthfully declared from all sources.
3. The Foundation and the school may verify the information.
4. False information will result in disqualification and full
   fees becoming payable.
5. Bursary awards are subject to annual re-assessment.
6. The Foundation's decision is final.
7. By accepting, you agree to the Bursary Contract terms.
8. Financial information is used solely for the bursary
   process.
9. You acknowledge data protection rights described in the
   Privacy Notice.
10. A separate Bursary Contract governs award terms.

### Acceptance checkbox

A single tick-box reading **By clicking this box, I/we agree to
the terms and conditions of the declaration set out above. I/we
confirm that all information provided in this application is true
and accurate to the best of my/our knowledge.**

### Signature field

**On behalf of the applicant** *(required)* — type the full name
of the person accepting the declaration. This is your typed
signature. It must match a parent/guardian name listed in section
3.

### Warning note

An amber box at the bottom reminding you that submitting is a
legal commitment and that false information leads to
disqualification.

## Steps

1. Read every numbered point in the declaration.
2. Tick the **By clicking this box…** checkbox.
3. Type your full name into the **On behalf of the applicant**
   field.
4. Click **Submit Application** in the bottom bar.
5. A confirmation page appears — see [[22-submit-application]].

## Tips

- Only one parent needs to type their name — but by signing you
  confirm you're authorised to do so on behalf of the household.
- If you change your mind before clicking Submit, just navigate
  away — nothing is final until you submit.
