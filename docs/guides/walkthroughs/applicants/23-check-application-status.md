# 23 — Check the status of your application

Backlink: [[README#After submission]]

After you submit, the portal becomes a status-tracking tool. You
can sign in any time to see exactly where your application is in
the review process.

## Where to find it

From the dashboard, click **View Status** in the Quick actions
area — or go directly to the **Application Status** page from
the sidebar.

## What the page shows

### Reference and status card

A coloured card at the top with:

- Your **Reference** number (the same one shown on the submission
  confirmation page).
- The child's name.
- The assessment round (e.g. "2026/27").
- A coloured **status pill**: Draft, Submitted, Under Review,
  Paused, Completed, Qualifies, or Does Not Qualify.

### Progress timeline

A vertical timeline with four steps, each with the date it was
reached:

1. **Draft** — *Application started.* Filled in when you began.
2. **Submitted** — *Application submitted for review.* Filled in
   when you clicked Submit.
3. **Under Review** — *Financial assessment in progress.* Filled
   in when a Foundation assessor opens your file.
4. **Outcome Available** — *Assessment complete.* Filled in when
   the assessor finishes.

Reached steps are gold; unreached steps are grey and read
"Pending".

### Decision date notice (before outcome)

A blue banner telling you when decisions for your round are
expected (e.g. *"Decisions for the 2026/27 round are expected by
15 April 2026."*).

### Outcome card (once available)

When the outcome is ready, a card appears in green or rose:

- **Green** — *"Your application qualifies for a bursary."*
- **Rose** — *"Your application does not qualify at this time."*

See [[25-read-your-outcome]] for what comes next.

## What each status label means

| Label | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| **Draft** | You're still filling in the form. Not yet sent. |
| **Submitted** | You've submitted. Waiting for an assessor to pick it up. |
| **Under Review** | An assessor is actively working on your file. |
| **Paused** | The assessor is waiting on something — usually a missing document from you. Check your email for a request. |
| **Completed / Qualifies / Does Not Qualify** | The decision has been made. |

## Tips

- You'll always receive an email at the major milestones. The
  status page is a convenience, not a substitute for those emails.
- A status that hasn't changed for a while is normal during busy
  parts of the round. Contact the bursary team if you're
  concerned.
