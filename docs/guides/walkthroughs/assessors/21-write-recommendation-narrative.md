# 21 — Write the recommendation narrative

Backlink: [[README#Tab 3 — Writing the recommendation]]

The recommendation summary is the panel-facing detailed write-up. It
supplements the family synopsis (shorter, school-facing) with a fuller
narrative.

## Prerequisites

- Recommendation tab open, assessment `COMPLETED`.

## Steps

1. In the **Recommendation Details** card, scroll to **Recommendation
   Summary** (textarea, 6 rows, resizeable). Placeholder reads
   *"Detailed recommendation narrative for the panel…"*.
2. Compose the narrative. Useful structure:
   - **Summary of circumstances** — one paragraph echoing and
     expanding on the family synopsis.
   - **Justification for the bursary level** — why this award
     percentage is appropriate given the calculation.
   - **Anything unusual** — manual adjustments and their reasons, red
     flags ticked in the assessment, sibling considerations, panel
     directions.
   - **Recommendation** — one sentence stating Qualifies / Does Not
     Qualify and any conditions.
3. Click **Save Recommendation**.
4. After saving, the green status text *"Recommendation saved."* appears
   beside the button.

## Verification

- The summary persists across reload.
- The PDF generated for the school renders the full text under the
  Recommendation Summary heading
  ([[29-generate-pdf-for-application]]).

## Notes

- This field is **panel-facing first, school-facing second**. Keep
  language professional; assume it may be read by the family if they
  later request a subject access request under GDPR.
- The summary is not exposed in the spreadsheet export — only the
  family synopsis and the recommendation flags. If the school needs
  the full narrative, send the PDF.
