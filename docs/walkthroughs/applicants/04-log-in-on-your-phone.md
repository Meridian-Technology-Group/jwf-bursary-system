# 04 — Log in on your phone

Backlink: [[README#Getting started]]

The portal works on any modern phone (iOS Safari and Chrome,
Android Chrome). You can complete the whole application from a
phone if you prefer — handy for photographing paper documents on
the spot.

## Steps

1. Open the portal URL in your phone's browser (the same address
   you'd use on a laptop — the invitation email links to it).
2. The login page shows **Sign in to your account** with two
   fields: **Email address** and **Password**, and a **Sign in**
   button below.
3. Type your email and password, then tap **Sign in**.

## What changes on a small screen

- The left-hand sidebar that holds the section list on desktop
  becomes a **menu icon** at the top of the screen. Tap it to
  reveal the list of application sections.
- A **bottom bar** appears at the foot of each section with
  **Back** on the left and **Save and Continue** on the right.
- Long forms scroll naturally; the bottom bar stays visible.
- File-upload buttons let you choose **Take a photo** or **Choose
  from library** — see [[20-upload-from-phone-camera]].

## Tips

- Tap **Forgot password?** below the password field if you can't
  remember it — see [[03-reset-forgotten-password]].
- Add the portal to your home screen for one-tap access: in
  Safari, tap the share icon then **Add to Home Screen**; in
  Chrome, use the three-dot menu → **Add to Home screen**.
- Your work is saved per section, so you can fill in a section on
  your phone, then carry on later from a laptop — your progress
  syncs automatically.

## If something goes wrong

- **Page looks oddly zoomed or doesn't fit.** Pinch-to-zoom out, or
  rotate the phone to landscape.
- **Login won't accept your password.** Use [[03-reset-forgotten-password]].
- **Camera upload greyed out.** Make sure you've granted your
  browser permission to use the camera in your phone's settings.
