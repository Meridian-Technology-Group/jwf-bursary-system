# 06 — Section 1: Details of Child

Backlink: [[README#Filling in the application]]

This is the first section of the form. It captures the child the
bursary application is for, and it includes one document upload —
the **birth certificate**.

## Before you start

Have to hand:

- The child's birth certificate (PDF or photo).
- The child's place of birth (country).
- The child's current school name and the date they started there.

## Steps

The form is split into five sub-sections, separated by horizontal
lines on the page.

### School selection

1. **School you are applying for** *(required)* — choose **Trinity
   School** or **Whitgift School**.
2. **School year your child is applying to enter** *(required)* —
   Year 6, Year 7, Year 9, Year 12, or "Other" (contact the bursary
   team if Other).

### Child information

3. **Child's full name** *(required)* — enter the child's full
   legal name exactly as it appears on the birth certificate.
4. **Gender** *(required)* — Male, Female, Prefer not to say, or
   Other.
5. **Date of birth** *(required)* — DD/MM/YYYY.
6. **Place of birth** *(required)* — search and pick the country.

### Birth certificate

7. Upload the birth certificate into the **Birth Certificate**
   slot. It must show the child's name, date of birth, place of
   birth, and parents' names. See [[16-upload-a-document]] for the
   mechanics. Accepted formats: PDF, JPG, PNG, up to 20 MB.

### Child's current address

8. **Does the child live at the same address as Parent/Guardian
   1?** *(required)* — Yes or No.
9. If **No**, fill in the child's address: Address line 1
   *(required)*, Address line 2, City / Town *(required)*,
   Postcode *(required)*, Country *(required)*.

### Current school

10. **School currently attended** *(required)* — the school the
    child is enrolled at right now.
11. **Start date at current school** *(required)*.

## Saving

When you reach the bottom of the page, click **Save and
Continue**. The system validates the form first — if any required
fields are missing, an error summary appears at the top and the
offending fields are highlighted in red. Fix them, then click
again. On success you'll be taken to section 2.

## Tips

- Names on the birth certificate, in this section, and in section 3
  (parents) should match — assessors compare them.
- You can leave this section partway through; come back later with
  **Continue Application** from the dashboard.
