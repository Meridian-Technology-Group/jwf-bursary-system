# 11 — Section 6: Other Information

Backlink: [[README#Filling in the application]]

A short section that covers three optional topics — court orders
for school fees, education-related insurance policies, and
outstanding school fees from previous years.

If none apply to your family, this section is just three "No"
answers.

## Steps

### Court orders

1. **Do you have a court order for the payment of school fees?**
   *(required)* — Yes or No.
2. If **Yes**:
   - **Amount per term** *(required)*.
   - **Amount per year** *(required)*.
   - You'll be asked to upload **Evidence of Court Order**.

### Insurance policies

3. **Do you have any insurance policies for the payment of school
   fees?** — Yes or No.
4. If **Yes**, enter the policy details (provider, annual amount,
   maturity date) and upload the policy schedule when prompted.

### Outstanding school fees

5. **Do you have any outstanding school fees from previous years?**
   — Yes or No.
6. If **Yes**, enter the amount owed and to which school.

### Save

7. Click **Save and Continue**.

## Tips

- Court-order amounts: enter the gross amount you receive (or pay).
  The assessor handles the in/out direction.
- Insurance "education plans" or savings plans earmarked for fees
  count — declare them.
