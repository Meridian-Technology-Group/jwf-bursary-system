# 21 — The pre-submission review page

Backlink: [[README#Before you submit]]

After section 9 (Additional Information), you land on the
**Review Your Application** page. This is the last checkpoint
before the legal declaration — it shows everything you've entered,
flags anything missing, and unlocks the Submit button when (and
only when) all required items are in place.

## What's on the page

### Progress bar

A horizontal bar at the top showing the percentage of required
items completed.

### Issues panel (if any items are missing)

A red box headed **N issues to resolve before you can submit**.
Each issue is listed as a row with a short description and a
**Go fix this** link that takes you straight to the offending
field in the offending section.

### "Looks complete" banner (if no issues)

A green box reading **Looks complete — review your details below
and proceed to the Declaration when ready.**

### Application sections

A numbered list of all nine data-entry sections (1 through 9),
each on its own card showing:

- The section title.
- A status pill: **Complete** (green), **Needs attention** (red),
  or **Not started** (grey).
- An **Edit** button that opens the section so you can change
  anything.
- A summary of what you entered.

### Ready to proceed? CTA

At the bottom is a card titled **Ready to proceed?**:

- **If there are blocking issues** — a red message saying "You
  have N unresolved issues" and a **Proceed to Declaration**
  button that's greyed out and disabled.
- **If everything is in order** — a calm message saying "All
  required information looks complete." and a clickable **Proceed
  to Declaration** button.

## Steps

1. Read the issues panel (if any). Click each **Go fix this** link
   in turn and the system jumps to the right field in the right
   section. Fix it, save, and you'll come back to the review page.
2. Once the panel disappears (replaced by the green "Looks
   complete" banner), scan the section cards to be sure your
   answers look right.
3. To change anything, click **Edit** on a section card.
4. When you're ready, click **Proceed to Declaration**.

## Why is the Submit button still disabled?

Common reasons:

- A required field is empty in one of the sections.
- A required document is missing from a slot.
- A conditional field appeared (because you answered Yes
  somewhere) and you didn't fill it in.

Every reason will show up in the **Issues panel** at the top. If
the panel is empty but the button is still disabled, refresh the
page — saved data may not have synced yet.
