# 27 — Complete a re-assessment

Backlink: [[README#Year two and beyond — re-assessment]]

A re-assessment is essentially the same form as the first-year
application, with two big differences: Section 2 (Family
Identification) is hidden, and the financial sections start blank.

> Note: the re-assessment-specific UI (skipped section, pre-fill
> banners) is in active development. The general flow below is
> the intended experience.

## What's pre-filled

Sections that don't change much year-on-year come pre-populated:

- **Section 1 — Details of Child** — school, child name, date of
  birth, place of birth, current school (the Foundation knows
  these from last year). Just check them and update anything
  that's wrong.
- **Section 3 — Parent / Guardian Details** — names, addresses,
  contact numbers. Update anything that's changed.
- **Section 4 — Dependent Children** — siblings carry over. Add
  any new ones, remove any who've left home.
- **Section 5 — Dependent Elderly** — same — confirm or amend.

You'll see a small **"Pre-filled from last year — please
confirm"** banner at the top of each pre-filled section.

## What starts blank

The financial picture changes every year, so:

- **Section 6 — Other Information** — re-answer Yes/No, re-enter
  amounts.
- **Section 7 — Parents' Income** — enter the new year's gross
  income for each parent and upload new P60s and other evidence.
- **Section 8 — Assets & Liabilities** — re-enter property
  values, savings, debts, and upload the latest council tax bill
  and three months of bank statements.
- **Section 9 — Additional Information** — flag any new
  circumstances; mention anything notable that happened this year.

## What's not asked

- **Section 2 — Family Identification.** The Foundation already
  has everyone's ID from last year. If a new dependant joined the
  family this year, contact the bursary team — they may ask for
  ID for the new person off-portal.

## Steps

1. Accept the invitation (see [[26-accept-reassessment-invitation]]).
2. Walk through each section. The sidebar shows nine entries
   instead of ten (Family ID is hidden).
3. For pre-filled sections, click into each, confirm the values,
   and click **Save and Continue**.
4. For the financial sections, fill them in fresh — see
   [[12-section-7-parents-income]] and [[13-section-8-assets-liabilities]].
5. Reach the review page, fix any issues, and submit just as
   you did in year one — see [[21-pre-submission-review-page]]
   and [[22-submit-application]].

## Tips

- Set aside an evening to gather the new financial documents
  before you start the form — that's the bulk of the work.
- Don't blank-out pre-filled fields by accident. If you do, the
  section flips back to "Needs attention" until you re-confirm.
