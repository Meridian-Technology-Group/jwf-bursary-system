# 32 — Bursary awards

Backlink: [[README#Reports]]

Distribution of recommended bursary award percentages across
applications in the selected round.

## Prerequisites

- Signed in as `ASSESSOR`, `ADMIN`, or `VIEWER`.
- A round with at least one saved recommendation.

## Steps

1. Sidebar → **Reports** (`/reports`).
2. Pick the round in the top-right round selector. The URL becomes
   `/reports?roundId=…`.
3. Use the sticky in-page section nav to jump to **Award
   Distribution**, or scroll directly to the **Award Distribution**
   card.
4. Read the strapline: *"Distribution of recommended bursary award
   percentages across all applications with a recommendation."*
5. The horizontal bar chart shows count per band (e.g. `0–25%`,
   `26–50%`, …, `91–100%`). Bands `91–100%` and `76–90%` are
   highlighted (primary navy) to make the higher-award tail
   immediately legible.
6. Hover any bar to see the count + percentage of total.

## Verification

- Total of band counts equals the round's completed-recommendation
  count.
- When the round has no recommendations, the card shows the empty
  state *"No data for this round yet"*.

## Notes

- "Bursary award" here is the % of gross fees covered, not the £
  bursary amount. For £-totals and averages, see the **School
  Comparison** card on the same page.
- Trend across rounds is not currently in this section; track via the
  per-round drill-down (change the round selector) and compare values
  externally.
